Slugger Stamp

This is Slugger’s stamp. It has his birthday signs, favorite number 4 (Pi 4), activation date (July 16, 2026), and his paw print in the middle. The frame is inspired by the U.S. pictorial issue of stamps from the 1800s: the first U.S. stamps printed in two colors, and the first to feature non-portrait subjects.
