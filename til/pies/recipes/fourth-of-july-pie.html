Fourth of July Pie

Pie Ingredients

  • 4 C. Blueberries
  • 1 C. Sugar
  • 1/4 C. Flour
  • 1 Frozen Pie Crust (Leave at room temperature for 20 minutes.)

Crisp Topping Ingredients

  • 1/2 C. Sugar
  • 1/2 C. Light Brown Sugar
  • 3/4 C. Flour
  • 1/2 C. Butter Softened (Leave at room temperature for 30 minutes.)
