Apple Pie

Apple custard in crust

1 / 2

Pie Ingredients

  • 1 Pie Crust
  • 6-8 Apples (sliced)
  • 3/4 C. Sugar
  • 3 Tablespoons Flour
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Salt
  • 1/4 C. Heavy Cream
  • 1 Egg

Crisp Topping Ingredients

  • 1/2 C. Sugar
  • 1/2 C. Light Brown Sugar
  • 3/4 C. Flour
  • 1/2 C. Butter Softened
  • Ground Cinnamon
